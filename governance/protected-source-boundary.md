# Prime Protected Source Boundary

Do not commit:

- passwords, keys, tokens, MFA or recovery codes;
- private keys or seed phrases;
- real `.env` values;
- PHI or raw medical records;
- raw finance, brokerage, tax, legal, insurance, mortgage, estate, or account evidence;
- IP addresses, private network maps, device registers, private runtime paths, or raw exports;
- unredacted household or infrastructure logs.

Prime may store clean pointers, schemas, protocols, runbooks, non-secret summaries, and sanitized evidence classifications.
